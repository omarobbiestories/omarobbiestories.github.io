Mandela Effect

A reflective composition

“Anti-social behaviour is a trait of intelligence in a world full of conformists… The gift of mental power comes from the Divine, and if we concentrate our minds on that truth, we become in tune with this great power.”

In 2009, paranormal consultant Fiona Broome coined the term Mandela Effect (ME), describing the conflict between people’s memories of the past and their current reality.

The Mandela Effect describes a phenomenon where large groups of people share the same or similar memories that do not match documented history.

Before Fiona Broome, a researcher named Starfire Tor had coined the term “Time Shift Effect”, also describing what she called the “Time Shift Living Dead Phenomena”.
