A Beginning

In 2009, paranormal consultant Fiona Broome coined the term Mandela Effect (ME), describing the conflict between people’s memories of the past and their current reality.

The Mandela Effect describes a phenomenon where large groups of people share the same or similar memories that do not match documented history.

Before Fiona Broome, a researcher named Starfire Tor had coined the term “Time Shift Effect”.
