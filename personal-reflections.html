Personal Reflections

Moments of thought and feeling — written as they came, shaped lightly for clarity.

These reflections sit somewhere between diary and prose — written in moments, shaped lightly, and left mostly as they were.

Reflection

Are we to believe that this is all there is?

Embrace that which is both a rose and a thorn—the miracle of the Christ-conscious dawn. Trying to live within a peaceful construct of cosmic interactions as I stand here with my simple mind.

The removal of obstacles.
The never-ending game.
The Christmas feast.
The creation of the beast.

This material world—buy, consume—the mental face of mindless interactions.

We are but reflections in the ocean of time—the flow. Believe in yourself.

The setting sun rises in the morning on the horizon, glowing with the rays of Christ’s cosmic energy, enriching us within another day of eternity.

Breathe in the greatness—rejuvenate, regenerate, grow, flow—with God’s creative energy. Cosmic interactions. Universal consciousness. Rivers of eternity flowing within expressions of time.

All truly are one within Christ, existing within this cosmic reality of consciousness.

This land of extremes—judgement, mindless adventures, revenge and reconciliation.

I’m in love with the idea of love. I’m in love with a feeling—with an experience.

Have I been brainwashed? Are these the ramblings of a sane person living in an insane world, or an insane person adjusting to a world of sanity? Deciphering the code of illusion and the clouds of disassociation.

My heart broke—living the illusion that I was here when I was there.

I stand somewhere, watching, observing—in my mind, yet out of my mind.

We cope. We do what we do. We deal with it. It matters—we learn to let go.

I see a little spider walking up the side of a drawer with a glass front in my room— this seems relevant to me somehow.

I praise my God, the Almighty. I give thanks for my life—for my parents, children, brothers, sisters, family and friends. Obstacles will come and go.

At times I am afraid—afraid of what I could possibly be. I look at the sky, I look at the stars, I look at the web I’ve created in my mind’s eye.

We are a reflection of the ocean—we are the flow.

Don’t hide in this moment of existence. Live.

To understand the space that is love—feel the heartbeat, hear the words, reflect upon silence. To understand that we may never understand the mystery; to continue searching, following the wind as it travels through the eye of the storm—and knowing, just knowing, that we are not meant to be ‘the norm’.

This is a frequency: loving, letting go—the goal. Eventually.

And when the sun shines overhead—we might see Christ, the Son, lighting our path.

Discipline.

The One.

Love

To those filled with the Spirit of God, who hear and move with its rhythm, we are like birds carrying words of love — reaching out through poems, songs, and verses to souls that may feel cold and empty.

Love is a creative force. It lifts us when we fall and steadies us through heartache. It is a fresh breath, a sense of belonging — the answer. Without love, the earth and stars would mean nothing. There would be no songs of hope, no reason for the flower to call the bee. Without love, there would be no meaning to our existence.

All I love has been given. This is our beginning: sharing our lives and holding each other steadfast.

Life shapes us — like a potter with clay, like a seed becoming a tree. With God, sorrow softens, and love endures. In that, warmth returns.

Our lives are linked, our futures unfolding through time. We rise like doves, reaching toward the stars, united in Christ’s love.

There are moments I still search and wonder, unsure of the path. Yet even there, love remains — bringing me back to what is true.

Time passes, and we grow older. Love holds memory and presence together, steady beneath it all.

In quiet reflection, I see what matters — my children, my family, those I hold dear — living within me always, held together by love.

I hold gratitude in my heart. Your love, my Lord, brings peace, strength, and freedom. You are always with us.

All I see, I see because of you.

All I feel, I feel because of you.

All I am, I am because of you.

In awe, my faith becomes new.
