Releasing

Poetry Collection

By Robin K

A collection of reflective poems exploring grief, healing, illusion, love, memory, awakening, and the releasing of what no longer serves us.

This poetry page is still growing — images and further reflections may be added over time.

Awaken

The high moon watches as souls leave bodies
Self
Life, birth, death — All Seeing Being creation
Energy
Interactions of life within the cosmic consciousness fabric
Dynamic
Reprogramming of the mind to alternate perceptions
Impermanence
The Oneness of Universal creation’s most grand perfection
Awakening

Broken

It was so very long ago
When we all said our goodbyes
Loved by our family we cried
A beautiful rose plucked so soon
Grief-stricken, we painfully buried you
In the garden of God’s love
We moved on with broken hearts

Contracts

Life is too short to hold grudges
Death is too close to escape
The beginning is always so sweet

Hidden by masks of illusion
Darkness slowly consumes the light

The dark self of destruction waits
Ready to create seas of frustration
As people carry coffins of memories

Lovesick graveyards of faded dreams
Fateful voyages once so innocent

Unload the cross of conditioned shame
Hypnotic words lead betrayed minds astray
Free yourself from the destructive cycles

Darling

We took a ride, sweet darling
We had the time of our lives
Living each day to its fullest
Laughing and crying, we did it all
Travelling far and wide, always satisfied
Now we’re old, sweetheart, time moved on
From when we were so young
Yesterday life took your last breath away
Time cannot take our unconditional love
Dearest one, to the grave you go
Our memories will always live on
We were like the sun and moon
We were like fire and rain
It is a barren land without you

Desire

Life is good and sometimes bad

Moments of peace and then confusion
The life of yesterday and today

To exist in life yet not care
People floating with illusions of self-pity
Emotional death in a world of plenty
Deception and pretence bind humans to destruction

To exist and be aware
Freedom for the captive and slave
Memory fades of heartache and pain
Knowledge that Creator’s love fulfils desire

Diversity

Expressions of cultural diversity
Individuality, freedom of choice
Interacting lives, unique thoughts
The dancing worldly stage
Mother Nature all around
Golden moments of time
The cloak of duality
Freedom for the mind
Release desire and temptation
Stand free and escape

An emotional price, mentally unwell

Alone in time, drinking the crime

Eyesight

You’re hidden from eyesight
Lost in a maze, a dark creepy place
Deep wells of suffering
Release these souls from lost and lonely sleeps
Lord, remove their illusions
Your holy voice falls silent upon deafened ears
Humble prayers to Oneness
Broken hearts lay resting in cold trembling hands
Your brilliant face appears
Your voice as clear as a nightingale’s song
From darkness to light
You free the imprisoned souls trapped in life
Offering insight to existence
Liberated from the darkness to live in light

Fearing

The repercussions of not letting go
Holding onto a stagnating existence
Ignoring the deafening frequencies to progress
Blocking doors to self-realisation
Fearful repercussions — everything stays the same
Poisoned thoughts suffocate comatose minds
The demanding ego relentlessly controlling lives
Sorrow invades like a thief
Stealing part of yourself far away
Reflect on our eternal Oneness
Release your greatness, true Self exists
Feel the freedom of releasing

Journey

Let go of the past, live today
Holding your heart on your sleeve
Allowing many moments to pass your way

Throw away your inner baggage, desires galore
Renew, start afresh, revitalize and restore

Freedom is to begin another journey

Storms raging within
Whirlpools of anger

Releasing

Let go of memories that bind you
A maze of emotionally confusing feelings
Restrictive obsessions, weak undisciplined minds
Confining imprisonment of self-doubt

Let go of anger and destructive thoughts
A masquerade of makeup and wardrobe
Degrading morality and selfish natures
Dishonest desires and expectations

Let go of grief, heartbreak and nightmares
Release negative memories, attachments and ego
The blinding mirror of vanity
Freedom is the reality

Let go of the past and yesterday
Remove the draining, suffocating emotional energy
Feel release, no more chains
Binding to the past

Let go of the restrictive social demands
Life is a change in motion
Release I, my and mine
Live in the moment

Time

Silence can be a deafening noise
Loneliness an imprisonment
People advise how to break free
Suffocating stressful tensions
Entrapped souls unable to perceive change
Breaking depressive bondage
Inside the memories blinding the mind
Answers to find
Change the habits and live freely
Find the path
To love our all-defining time

Reflection on Releasing

Releasing is not forgetting. It is not pretending pain did not happen, nor denying grief, love, memory, longing, or regret.

It is the gradual unburdening of what has held the spirit captive: sorrow, illusion, fear, anger, self-doubt, expectation, and emotional exhaustion.

To release is to loosen the grip of what keeps the heart and mind bound to old wounds. It is to move more freely, more honestly, and more gently into the present moment.

These poems reflect on loss, awakening, struggle, remembrance, and the possibility of inner freedom. Together they form a quiet meditation on change, consciousness, suffering, and the hope of renewal.
