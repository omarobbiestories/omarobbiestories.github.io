Releasing

A lifetime of observation

By Robin K

A reflective collection on letting go of grief, change and healing

This collection may continue to grow with additional poems and images.

Awaken

Conscious energy observes
souls existing in form.

Self.

Life. Birth. Death.
All-seeing creation.

Love.

Life dancing within
the fabric of time.

Consciousness.

Reality. Illusion.
Impermanence. Oneness.

Awakening.

Broken

Long ago, we said our goodbyes.
Loved by your family, we cried.

A beautiful rose, plucked too soon,
grief-stricken, we laid you to rest.

In the garden of God’s love,
we carried on — living life’s test.

Contracts

Life is too short for grudges
Death too close to escape
Beginnings always sweet

Masked in illusion
Darkness consuming light

Self-destruction awaits
Rising above seas of frustration
As we carry coffins of memory

Graveyards of faded dreams
Voyages once innocent

Lay down the cross of conditioned shame
Hypnotic words lead minds astray
Break free of the cycle
